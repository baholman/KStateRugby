About K-State Ft. Riley Rugby

The Kansas State/Fort Riley Rugby Club (KSUFR) competes in Division 1AA Collegiate rugby.
KSUFR is the 2017 Heart of America 7's League Champions, and the 2009-2010 and 2010-2011 Central Division champions.
The team plays league matches in the Heart of America RFU against Iowa State, Kansas, Missouri, Nebraska, Oklahoma State,
Truman State and Washington University in St. Louis. KSUFR also play Club Matches against area teams.


Practices

Our Practices are held at the World War I Memorial Stadium at Kansas State University.
KSUFR typically only practices during the fall and spring semesters.
The practice typically occur 6-8pm Tuesday through Thursday. However, the time and
practice location may change do to weather or other factors. To contact one of our officers
for a current update on our practice schedule, click here.
